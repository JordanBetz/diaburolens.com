About Me

Welcome to my photography portfolio! I am passionate about capturing beautiful moments and creating stunning visual stories through my lens.

When I attended DoKomi, I never expected to discover my passion for photography. It was a fortuitous event where I had the opportunity to borrow my father's DSLR camera. Although I was hesitant to take many photos, being a novice in this field, I had an incredible amount of fun capturing the magic of unique cosplays, immersing myself in the vibrant atmosphere, and capturing captivating details that immediately sparked my interest.

From that moment on, my curiosity and enthusiasm for photography grew steadily. I started delving deeper into the technical aspects and possibilities of camera technology. Portrait photography fascinated me with its ability to capture the unique features and emotions of a person, while street photography immersed me in the pulsating world of the streets.

I attended workshops, delved into books, and spent hours admiring the works of inspiring photographers. Each time I pressed the shutter and captured the perfect moment, I felt an incredible joy and satisfaction.

With a newfound love for cosplay photography, I am excited to embark on a journey of attending numerous anime conventions. These gatherings provide a fertile ground for me to capture the essence of countless remarkable cosplayers, each expressing their unique interpretation of beloved characters. I am eager to immerse myself in this dynamic community, to collaborate with cosplayers, and to capture their extraordinary transformations.

My love for photography extends to the captivating world of cosplay, where I find endless inspiration.

Each convention offers a new opportunity to connect with cosplayers, learn from their creativity, and capture the magic that unfolds when fans come together to celebrate their shared love for anime and cosplay. I am excited to capture many more unique moments, to tell the stories of these talented cosplayers, and to share the joy and inspiration they bring to the world through my photographs.

Photography has become my personal journey of self-expression and creative exploration, and the world of cosplay has added a vibrant and enchanting dimension to it. I am filled with passion and determination to continue growing as a photographer, refining my skills, and sharing the beauty and artistry of cosplay through my photographs.

Although I am still at the beginning of my photographic journey, I am filled with passion and determination to continue growing. Each day offers new opportunities to refine my craft and expand my skills. Photography has opened up a wonderful world for me, and I look forward to capturing many more unique moments and sharing them with others.
